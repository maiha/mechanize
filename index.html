mechanize by maiha

Mechanize is a ruby library that makes automated web interaction easy.

Fork of tenderlove/mechanize * break Form::Field into Form::Text,Hidden,Textarea * breaks Form::Button into Submit,Reset * Form::Image,Label and Form::RadioButton#text that traces its label * Page#images
